Iron Man 3 (2013)

Tony Stark confronts his past and a new technological threat in this tense, personal sequel.

Movie Overview

After the events of The Avengers, Tony Stark struggles with PTSD and the challenges of being Iron Man. A new enemy emerges in the form of a mysterious terrorist called the Mandarin, and Stark must rely on his ingenuity to protect those he loves.

Critical Reception

Iron Man 3 was praised for its performances (especially Downey), inventive set pieces, and emotional beats, though some fans debated the Mandarin twist. Overall, it was a box-office success and a satisfying close to Tony Stark's trilogy arc at that time.
