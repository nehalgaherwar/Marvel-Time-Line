Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

Movie Overview

When Tony Stark and Bruce Banner try to jump-start a dormant peacekeeping program, things go awry and the villainous Ultron emerges, intent on wiping out humanity. The Avengers must unite to stop him and save the world from destruction.
