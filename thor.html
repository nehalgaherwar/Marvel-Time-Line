Thor (2011)

The Norse god is banished to Earth and must prove himself worthy.

Movie Overview

After reigniting an ancient war, the mighty Thor is cast out of the fantastic realm of Asgard by his father Odin and sent to live among humans on Earth. Stripped of his powers, Thor must learn what it takes to be a true hero when the most dangerous villain of his world sends the darkest forces of Asgard to invade Earth.
