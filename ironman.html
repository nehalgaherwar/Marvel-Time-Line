Critical Reception

Iron Man received widespread critical acclaim, with praise directed at Downey's performance, the film's action sequences, visual effects, and humor. The film was selected by the American Film Institute as one of the ten best films of 2008 and received two nominations at the 81st Academy Awards for Best Sound Editing and Best Visual Effects.
