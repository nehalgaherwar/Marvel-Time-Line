Movie Overview

Set to the backdrop of Awesome Mixtape #2, the Guardians of the Galaxy continue their adventures as they traverse the outer reaches of the cosmos. The team must help Peter Quill uncover the truth about his mysterious parentage while dealing with new threats and keeping their newfound family together. Along the way, old foes become new allies and fan-favorite characters from the classic comics come to our heroes' aid.
