Movie Overview

After a tragic car accident that destroys his surgical career, the brilliant but arrogant Doctor Stephen Strange discovers a world of mystic arts. Under the Ancient One's tutelage, Strange must learn to put aside his ego and protect the world from mystical threats, particularly the zealous Kaecilius who seeks eternal life through the Dark Dimension.
