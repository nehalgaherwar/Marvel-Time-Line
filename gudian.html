Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

A ragtag team of misfits comes together to save the galaxy with heart and humor.

Movie Overview

After stealing a mysterious orb, Peter Quill finds himself hunted by the villainous Ronan. To evade capture, Quill forms an uneasy alliance with a group of extraterrestrial misfits: Gamora, Rocket, Drax, and Groot. Together they must work as a team to protect the galaxy.
