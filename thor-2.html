Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Thor faces a cosmic threat as the Dark Elves return to plunge the universe into darkness.

Movie Overview

Thor reunites with Jane Foster and teams up with his treacherous brother Loki to stop Malekith and the Dark Elves from using the Aether to plunge the universe into darkness. The fate of the Nine Realms hangs in the balance as Thor faces his most personal and cosmic battle yet.
