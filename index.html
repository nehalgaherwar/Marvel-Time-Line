Marvel Cinematic Universe Movies Timeline

This page lists all major MCU theatrical films in release order, organized by phases. Each entry includes the release year and a short, spoiler-free summary of the story. Click on a movie title for more details!

Phase 1 (2008-2012)

Iron Man (2008)

Tony Stark, a brilliant inventor and weapons manufacturer, builds a high-tech suit of armor to escape captivity and embarks on a path to becoming a superhero.

The Incredible Hulk (2008)

Scientist Bruce Banner struggles to control his transformations into the Hulk after a failed experiment, while pursued by a military force seeking to weaponize him.

Iron Man 2 (2010)

Tony Stark faces government pressure to share his Iron Man technology and battles a rival inventor with a grudge against his family's legacy.

Thor (2011)

The Norse god Thor is banished to Earth by his father Odin and must reclaim his hammer Mjolnir while defending humanity from his brother Loki's schemes.

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

In WWII, scrawny Steve Rogers undergoes a super-soldier experiment to become Captain America and fights the villainous Red Skull in a battle for the world.

The Avengers (2012)

Nick Fury assembles Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye to stop Loki and an alien invasion threatening Earth.

Phase 2 (2013-2015)

Iron Man 3 (2013)

Tony Stark deals with PTSD after the Avengers battle and faces a terrorist known as the Mandarin, uncovering a conspiracy that tests his ingenuity.

Thor: The Dark World (2013)

Thor battles the Dark Elves, led by Malekith, who seek to plunge the universe into darkness using the reality-altering Aether.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

Steve Rogers uncovers a conspiracy within S.H.I.E.L.D. involving the shadowy Winter Soldier, forcing him to question his loyalties.

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

Peter Quill and a ragtag group of misfits—a raccoon, a tree, Gamora, and Drax—band together to stop a powerful orb from falling into the wrong hands.

Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

The Avengers face Ultron, Tony Stark's rogue AI creation, which aims to evolve humanity by wiping it out, leading to new alliances and losses.

Ant-Man (2015)

Ex-con Scott Lang dons a suit that shrinks him to insect size and teams up with Hank Pym to pull off a heist against a dangerous new threat.

Phase 3 (2016-2019)

Captain America: Civil War (2016)

The Avengers fracture over accountability issues, pitting Iron Man against Captain America in a conflict involving a brainwashed super-soldier.

Doctor Strange (2016)

Arrogant surgeon Stephen Strange learns mystic arts from the Ancient One to battle interdimensional threats and protect reality.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

The Guardians discover Peter Quill's mysterious father and face ego-driven dangers while strengthening their found family bonds.

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

Teenage Peter Parker balances high school life with being Spider-Man, taking on the vulture-like scavenger Adrian Toomes.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

Thor teams up with Loki and Hulk to save Asgard from the fiery prophet Hela and her quest for conquest.

Black Panther (2018)

T'Challa becomes king of Wakanda and the Black Panther, defending his nation from internal and external threats seeking vibranium.

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

The Avengers and their allies must be willing to sacrifice all in an attempt to defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe.

Phase 4 (2020-2023)

Black Widow (2020)

Natasha Romanoff confronts the darker parts of her ledger when a dangerous conspiracy with ties to her past arises.

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (2021)

Shang-Chi, the master of unarmed weaponry-based Kung Fu, is forced to confront his past after being drawn into the Ten Rings organization.

Eternals (2021)

The Eternals, ancient aliens with superhuman powers, have secretly protected humanity for thousands of years, but now face a new threat.

Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021)

Peter Parker seeks help from Doctor Strange to make everyone forget his secret identity, but things go wrong and he must face villains from other universes.

Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022)

Doctor Strange teams up with a mysterious teenage girl from his dreams who can travel across the multiverse, her powers becoming the most dangerous when she becomes possessed by a powerful entity.

Thor: Love and Thunder (2022)

Thor embarks on a journey unlike anything he's ever faced -- a quest for inner peace. But his retirement is interrupted by a galactic killer known as Gorr the God Butcher.

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever (2022)

The people of Wakanda fight to protect their home from intervening world powers as they mourn the death of King T'Challa.

Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania (2023)

Scott Lang and Hope van Dyne are dragged into the Quantum Realm, along with Hope's parents and Scott's daughter Cassie, where they encounter a new strange creatures and embark on an adventure that will push them beyond the limits of what they thought was possible.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3 (2023)

Peter Quill, still reeling from the loss of Gamora, must rally his team around him to defend the universe along with protecting one of their own.

Phase 5 (2023-2025)

The Marvels (2023)

Carol Danvers gets her powers entangled with those of Kamala Khan and Monica Rambeau, forcing them to work together to save the universe.

Deadpool & Wolverine (2024)

Deadpool is offered a place in the Marvel Cinematic Universe by the Time Variance Authority, but instead recruits a variant of Wolverine to save his universe from extinction.

Captain America: Brave New World (2024)

Sam Wilson, the new Captain America, finds himself in the middle of an international incident and must discover the price of becoming an American hero.

Thunderbolts* (2024)

A group of anti-heroes are recruited by the government to undertake black ops missions.

Blade (2024)

Blade is a half-vampire, half-human who becomes a hunter of vampires.

Fantastic Four (2024)

The Fantastic Four are a team of superheroes who gain their powers from cosmic rays.

Avengers: Doomsday (2025)

The Avengers must come together to stop Doctor Doom from destroying the world.

Avengers: Secret Wars (2025)

The Avengers face their greatest challenge yet as they battle across multiple universes.
