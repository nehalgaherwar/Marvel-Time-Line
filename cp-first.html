Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

Origin story of a hero forged in wartime and reborn for the modern age.

Movie Overview

During World War II, frail but determined Steve Rogers volunteers for an experimental program that transforms him into Captain America, a super-soldier who must stop the villainous Red Skull and the extra-terrestrial threat of HYDRA.
